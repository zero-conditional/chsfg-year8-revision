About this site

Where the information comes from

The exam dates, subject list, and revision focus on this site are taken from the school's Year 8 exam information for May 2026. The site then reorganises that information into pages that are easier for families to use.

What is on this site?

Core papers plus GL assessments

  • English: descriptive writing
  • Maths: calculator paper
  • Science: short-answer and data interpretation
  • GL English, GL Maths, and GL Science

What to revise first

Start with repeated-practice subjects

  • Maths and GL Maths reward regular question practice.
  • Science and GL Science benefit from facts plus data questions.
  • English improves through short planning and writing practice.
